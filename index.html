About synontech

Synontech is a solo digital studio built on the principle that size doesn't determine ambition. Operating across games, mobile apps, AI systems, and web platforms, every product is built with the same obsessive attention to craft.

From immersive cyberpunk simulations with AI-driven characters to lean productivity tools that solve real problems, synontech operates at the bleeding edge of what one person can build.

No committees. No compromise. Just fast iteration, clean code, and experiences that actually ship.
