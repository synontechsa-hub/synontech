Capabilities

Workshop Optimized

🔲

Smart Optimization

MaxRects BSSF and Guillotine algorithms chew through complex piece lists, finding the tightest possible packing arrangement across all your available sheets in seconds.

📋

Multi-Sheet Planning

Working with different sheet sizes in a single job? No problem. KerfCut tracks them all and prioritizes packing to minimize the total number of sheets required.

💰

Instant Quoting

Input your raw material costs and desired markup. KerfCut automatically calculates the total job price based on actual material usage, ready for client review.

🌾

Grain & Rotation Locks

Working with timber or veneer? Lock the grain direction per piece to ensure the optimizer respects your material's visual or structural requirements.

📄

PDF Cut Plans

Generate crisp, professional PDF reports containing visual layout diagrams, numbered piece lists, and cost breakdowns. Print them for the shop floor or attach them to quotes.

⚡

Asynchronous Processing

Heavy jobs with hundreds of pieces won't freeze the app. The optimization engine runs safely in the background, keeping the interface snappy and responsive.
