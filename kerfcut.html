KerfCut

Precision productivity for the modern maker. KerfCut simplifies complex workshop calculations, inventory tracking, and project planning into a high-performance mobile interface.

The Mission

Built for craftspeople who value efficiency, KerfCut removes the friction between planning and execution. It's designed to be used in high-intensity workshop environments where speed and accuracy are non-negotiable.
